Enter your Apache User ID

Enter your Apache User ID and we will send a password reset email to your availid@apache.org address. The email will be encrypted to your public PGP key, if your profile identifies one.

If the email doesn't arrive, email root@ for assistance.

If you have lost access to your registered email address, you will need to file an additional ICLA with Secretary. Follow the directions for submitting an ICLA.

Include your current Apache id on the ICLA and mention in your cover email that you are requesting a change to your email address.
